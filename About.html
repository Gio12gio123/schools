About Us

We are a group of passionate 7th-grade students enrolled in the International Baccalaureate (IB) program, focusing on Technology, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship (MTIEBI). Our goal is to explore creative solutions, develop our problem-solving skills, and apply them to real-world challenges.

Our Mission

We are committed to building innovative projects that combine technology and creativity. Through hands-on learning, we aim to create solutions that address local and global issues, while collaborating and learning from each other.

What We Do

In the MTIEBI program, we explore new technologies, dive into innovative business ideas, and develop skills that prepare us for the future. Our projects include working with coding, robotics, environmental sustainability, and entrepreneurial thinking.

Our Values

We believe in teamwork, critical thinking, and constant curiosity. As 7th graders, we are excited to push the boundaries of what we know and create solutions that can make a real difference in the world.
